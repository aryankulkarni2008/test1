Maharashtra

Maharashtra is one of the largest states in India. Mumbai is the capital city. It is famous for forts, Ajanta-Ellora caves and rich culture.


Gujarat

Gujarat is famous for the Statue of Unity, Gir National Park and business industries. Gandhinagar is its capital city.


Rajasthan

Rajasthan is known for deserts, palaces and historical forts. Jaipur is its capital.


Karnataka

Karnataka is famous for Bengaluru, Mysore Palace and IT industries.
